Asteroids!

The Ship of Wonders!
Press W to accelerate
Press A to turn left
Press D to turn right
Press S to accelerate backward
Press Space for hyperspace
LEFT Clicking on the screen will create a barrier around the ship and will allow you to shoot bullets as well.
RIGHT Clicking on the screen will allow you to dodge asteroids. Dodging will stop you from shooting bullets (LEFT Click again to start shooting again).
The red health pack will heal you.
CAUTION: You may spawn into an asteroid and take damage at the start.
